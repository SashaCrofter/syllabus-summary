Chemistry 101 starts on January 26th and ends on May 24th. There is
class every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday from 9am to 9:50am. There are
3 exams, 10 homework assignments, and 12 quizzes. Exam 1 takes place on
February 14th, exam 2 takes place on March 2nd, and exam 3 takes place
on April 20th. There are 2 presentations and 2 projects that can be
turned in for extra credit. The extra credit due dates will be announced
in class.
